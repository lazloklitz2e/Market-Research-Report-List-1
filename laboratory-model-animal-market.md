<p><p>The laboratory model animal market consists of various types, including homozygote, heterozygote, and genetically modified animals. Homozygote animals possess identical alleles at specific gene loci, ensuring consistency in experimental results. Heterozygote animals have diverse alleles, allowing researchers to study variable traits and gene interactions. Genetically modified animals are engineered to exhibit specific genetic alterations, enabling advanced applications in disease research and drug development. Together, these models provide essential tools for biomedical research, enhancing our understanding of genetics and disease mechanisms.</p></p>
