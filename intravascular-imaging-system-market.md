<p><p>The intravascular imaging system market comprises various technologies, including Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) endoscopic imaging systems and hybrid intravascular imaging systems. OCT endoscopic imaging provides high-resolution, cross-sectional images of vascular structures, enhancing diagnostic precision and treatment planning. Hybrid systems combine multiple imaging modalities, such as intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) and OCT, allowing for comprehensive visualization and assessment of coronary arteries. This integration aids in better clinical outcomes through improved decision-making during interventional procedures and disease management.</p></p>
