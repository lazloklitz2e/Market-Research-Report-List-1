<p><p>Silicone additives encompass various types that enhance performance in multiple applications. Defoamers reduce foam formation during processing; wetting and dispersing agents improve the spreadability and stability of formulations; rheology modifiers adjust viscosity for optimal flow and stability; surfactants facilitate better interaction between different phases; and lubricating agents minimize friction, enhancing product longevity. Together, these silicone additives contribute to improved product quality, efficiency, and usability across industries such as coatings, plastics, and personal care.</p></p>
