<p><p>The multi-frequency GNSS receiver boards market is categorized into dual-frequency, tri-frequency, and other types. Dual-frequency receivers capture signals from two different GNSS bands, enhancing accuracy and mitigating errors from atmospheric disturbances. Tri-frequency receivers take this further by accessing three bands, providing even greater precision and reliability in diverse environments. Other market types may include specialized receivers that utilize customizable frequencies or focus on niche applications, catering to varying needs in industries like surveying, agriculture, and autonomous navigation.</p></p>
