<p><p>The macular edema therapeutics market includes various treatment modalities. Laser treatment involves using focused light to reduce fluid accumulation and improve vision. Anti-VEGF injections target vascular endothelial growth factor to diminish abnormal blood vessel growth and fluid leakage. Anti-inflammatory treatments aim to reduce inflammation and swelling in the retina. Vitrectomy is a surgical procedure that removes vitreous gel to relieve traction, potentially alleviating edema. Each approach addresses different underlying causes of macular edema, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
