<p><p>The stainless steel diaphragm valve market is segmented into electric and manual types. Electric diaphragm valves are operated via electric actuators, offering precise control and automation, making them ideal for remote operations and applications requiring consistent performance. In contrast, manual diaphragm valves are operated mechanically, allowing users to control flow through handwheels or levers. While manual valves are typically more cost-effective, electric options provide enhanced efficiency and safety in environments where quick response and automation are crucial.</p></p>
