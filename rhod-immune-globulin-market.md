<p><p>Rho(D) Immune Globulin is a blood product used primarily to prevent Rh alloimmunization in Rh-negative individuals, particularly during pregnancy or after blood transfusions. This product is crucial in obstetric care to prevent complications such as hemolytic disease of the newborn.</p><p>The Rho(D) Immune Globulin market is projected to witness steady growth, driven by increasing awareness of Rh incompatibility issues and rising prenatal care services.
