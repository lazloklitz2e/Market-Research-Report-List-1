<p><p>The artificial hearts and circulatory assist market primarily includes Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) and total artificial hearts. VADs are mechanical pumps that help the heart circulate blood, often used for patients with severe heart failure who are awaiting a transplant or are not candidates for surgery. In contrast, total artificial hearts completely replace the heart's function, providing a temporary or permanent solution for end-stage heart disease. Both technologies aim to improve patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
