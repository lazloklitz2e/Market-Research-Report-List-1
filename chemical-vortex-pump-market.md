<p><strong>The Chemical Vortex Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Vortex Pump</li><li>Open Vortex Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical vortex pump market is segmented into two main types: closed vortex pumps and open vortex pumps. Closed vortex pumps utilize a sealed design to create a vortex flow, which is effective for handling corrosive substances and providing high efficiency. Conversely, open vortex pumps allow for open fluid flow, often facilitating easier maintenance and handling of solids. Both types are essential in various industrial processes, particularly in chemical, wastewater, and pharmaceutical applications, catering to specific operational requirements and fluid characteristics.</p></p>
