<p><strong>The VoIP Gateways Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog VoIP Gateway</li><li>Digital VoIP Gateway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VoIP gateways serve as critical intermediaries between traditional telephony systems and Internet-based communication. Analog VoIP gateways connect standard telephone lines (POTS) to IP networks, enabling voice transmission over the internet while allowing legacy systems to interface with modern VoIP services. In contrast, digital VoIP gateways connect digital telephony systems, like ISDN or T1 lines, to IP networks, ensuring high-quality voice communication and scalability. Both types are essential for businesses transitioning from legacy phone systems to advanced VoIP solutions.</p></p>
