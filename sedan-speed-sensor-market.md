<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sedan speed sensor market can be categorized into two main types: active and passive sensors. Active sensors generate their own signal to measure speed, typically using electromagnetic or optical methods, offering higher accuracy and performance under various conditions. In contrast, passive sensors rely on external input, usually from the vehicle's transmission, to determine speed, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety, performance, and efficiency by accurately monitoring speed.</p></p>
