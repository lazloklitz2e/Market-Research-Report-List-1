<p><p>The solvent polyurethane market is categorized into three main types: Polyether, Polyester, and Mixed types. Polyether polyurethanes are known for their excellent hydrolysis resistance and flexibility, making them ideal for applications requiring durability in moisture-rich environments. Polyester polyurethanes offer superior mechanical properties and are more resistant to wear and tear, applicable in coatings and industrial uses. Mixed type polyurethanes combine features of both polyether and polyester, providing a versatile option that balances performance and cost-effectiveness for various applications.</p></p>
