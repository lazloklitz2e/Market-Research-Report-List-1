<p><p>The intracardiac imaging market encompasses various modalities crucial for cardiovascular assessments. Intracardiac echocardiography (ICE) is a minimally invasive technique offering real-time imaging of cardiac structures during procedures. Transthoracic echocardiography (TTE) is a non-invasive method commonly used to evaluate heart function and anatomy via the chest wall. Other imaging types may include transesophageal echocardiography and cardiac MRI, providing alternative insights into cardiac conditions. Together, these imaging modalities enhance diagnosis and treatment planning in cardiology.</p></p>
