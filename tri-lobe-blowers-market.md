<p><p>Tri-lobe blowers are classified into two primary market types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal tri-lobe blowers are designed with their lobes positioned horizontally, which optimizes airflow and efficiency for various applications. They are often used in industries requiring high flow rates. In contrast, vertical tri-lobe blowers have lobes oriented vertically, offering compact designs ideal for limited space environments. Both designs provide reliable performance for pneumatic conveying, aeration, and vacuum applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
