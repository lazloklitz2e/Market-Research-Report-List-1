<p><strong>The Manual Plug Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Way Plug Valve</li><li>Three Way Plug Valve</li><li>Four Way Plug Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual plug valve market is categorized into three main types: two-way, three-way, and four-way plug valves. Two-way plug valves control the flow in a single direction, often used for on/off applications. Three-way plug valves allow for flow diversion, enabling mixing or splitting of flow paths. Four-way plug valves manage multiple flow paths, facilitating more complex operations. Each type serves specific applications in various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing, enhancing operational efficiency and control.</p></p>
