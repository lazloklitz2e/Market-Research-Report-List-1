Various types include PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), known for its strength and durability; CPP (Cast Polypropylene), which offers good moisture resistance; BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene), favored for its clarity and printability; PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol), notable for its water solubility; PLA (Polylactic Acid), a biodegradable option; and Others, encompassing materials like nylon and EVOH, used for specialized applications. Each type caters to specific packaging needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
