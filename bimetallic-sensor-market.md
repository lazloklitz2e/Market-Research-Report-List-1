<p><p>The bimetallic sensor market is categorized into analog and digital types based on their output signals. Analog bimetallic sensors provide continuous output varying with temperature changes, making them suitable for applications requiring real-time monitoring. In contrast, digital bimetallic sensors convert temperature readings into discrete signals for improved accuracy and integration with digital systems. Each type serves different industrial needs, with analog sensors typically used in traditional systems, while digital sensors find favor in modern automation and smart applications.</p></p>
