<p><p>The Smart Card OEM/ODM market encompasses various security solutions, including One-Time Password (OTP) and Multifactor Authentication (MFA) systems. OTP solutions generate unique, time-sensitive passwords for secure access, enhancing user authentication for transactions or logins. In contrast, MFA combines multiple verification methods, such as PINs, biometrics, or smart cards, providing an additional security layer against unauthorized access. Both technologies play crucial roles in safeguarding sensitive information in sectors such as finance, healthcare, and government.</p></p>
