<p><p>The snow goggles market comprises three primary types: ordinary lenses, myopic lenses, and presbyopic glasses. Ordinary lenses cater to the general public, offering standard protection and visibility in snowy conditions. Myopic lenses are designed for individuals with nearsightedness, providing corrective vision while skiing or snowboarding. Presbyopic glasses cater to those experiencing age-related vision changes, allowing for a clear view at various distances during winter sports. Each lens type ensures safety and enhanced performance for outdoor enthusiasts.</p></p>
