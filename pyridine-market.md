<p><p>The pyridine market is primarily categorized into two types: chemically synthesized pyridine and coal tar extracted pyridine. Chemically synthesized pyridine is manufactured through various synthetic processes, offering high purity and consistent quality, making it suitable for pharmaceutical and agrochemical applications. In contrast, coal tar extracted pyridine is derived from the distillation of coal tar, often containing impurities, but is a more cost-effective option for industries such as rubber and dye production. Both types serve distinct market needs.</p></p>
