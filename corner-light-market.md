<p><ul><li>Halogen/Incandescent</li><li>Xenon/HID</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corner light markets primarily feature three types: Halogen/Incandescent, Xenon/HID, and LED. Halogen and incandescent lights offer warm illumination and simple technology but are less energy-efficient. Xenon/HID lights provide bright, intense illumination, ideal for high-performance applications, while being more energy-efficient than traditional bulbs. LED lights are the most efficient, offering long lifespans and versatility in designs, with various color temperatures and minimal heat generation. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences regarding light quality, energy consumption, and durability.</p></p>
