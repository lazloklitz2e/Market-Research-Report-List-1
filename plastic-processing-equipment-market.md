<p><p>The plastic processing equipment market encompasses various types of machinery essential for transforming plastic materials into finished products. Injection molding machinery is used to inject molten plastic into molds, allowing for complex shapes and high precision. Extruders machinery processes plastic into continuous shapes, such as films or pipes, by forcing it through a die. Blow molding machinery specifically forms hollow plastic parts by inflating molten plastic within a mold. Other equipment in this market includes thermoformers and cutting machines, vital for diverse applications.</p></p>
