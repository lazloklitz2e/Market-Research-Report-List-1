<p><p>The Emergency Shutoff Valves market primarily features two types: gate valves and ball valves. Gate valves provide a straightforward on/off function without minimizing pressure, making them ideal for fully opened or closed applications. Conversely, ball valves offer quick operation with a spherical disc that allows for rapid flow control and minimal resistance. Both valve types are crucial in emergency situations for controlling fluid flow, ensuring safety in various industries, including oil and gas, water supply, and chemical processing.</p></p>
