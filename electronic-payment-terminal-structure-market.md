<p><p>The electronic payment terminal market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Conversely, wireless terminals utilize technologies such as Wi-Fi or mobile networks, offering flexibility and mobility for on-the-go transactions, making them ideal for restaurants and delivery services. Both types aim to enhance transaction efficiency and customer experience while ensuring security in electronic payments.</p></p>
