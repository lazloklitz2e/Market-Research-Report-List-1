<p><p>The aluminum plates market is segmented into different series based on alloy composition, each offering unique properties for various applications. The 2XXX series, primarily aluminum-copper alloys, are known for high strength and are often used in aerospace. The 5XXX series, aluminum-magnesium alloys, offer excellent corrosion resistance, making them ideal for marine applications. The 6XXX series combines magnesium and silicon, providing good weldability, while the 7XXX series, mainly aluminum-zinc alloys, is sought after in aerospace. The 8XXX series includes other elements, typically used in packaging. Additionally, the 1XXX and 3XXX series focus on high conductivity and moderate strength properties, respectively.</p></p>
