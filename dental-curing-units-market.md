<p><p>Dental curing units are essential tools for hardening dental materials, primarily composites. The market is categorized into three main types: LED, UV, and halogen curing units. LED curing units utilize light-emitting diodes, offering efficiency and longer lifespan with less heat generation. UV curing units use ultraviolet light, ideal for specific materials but may have limited application due to safety concerns. Halogen curing units, the traditional option, provide broad-spectrum light but generate more heat and have a shorter lifespan compared to LEDs.</p></p>
