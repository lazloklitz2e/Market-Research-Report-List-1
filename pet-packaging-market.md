<p><p>The PET packaging market is primarily divided into two types: rigid and flexible packaging. Rigid packaging, made from solid PET, provides durability and protection, often used for bottles and containers. It offers excellent shelf life and consumer appeal. In contrast, flexible packaging utilizes thinner PET films, allowing for lightweight, adaptable solutions like pouches and wraps. This type is popular for its convenience, cost-effectiveness, and reduced material usage, making it increasingly favored in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
