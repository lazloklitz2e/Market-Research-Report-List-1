<p><strong>The Nuclear Molecular Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positron Emission Tomography</li><li>Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nuclear Molecular Imaging System market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Positron Emission Tomography (PET)** utilizes radiotracers to visualize metabolic processes in the body, offering detailed images for cancer detection and neurological disorders. </p><p>2. **Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT)** provides 3D imaging by detecting gamma rays, commonly used for cardiac and brain imaging. </p><p>3. **Planar Scintigraphy Systems** deliver 2D images based on gamma radiation, primarily for routine diagnostic applications in various medical fields, including oncology and cardiology.</p></p>
