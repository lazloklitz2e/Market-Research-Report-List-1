<p><p>The Sedan Ignition Coil market can be divided into single-spark and multi-spark types. Single-spark ignition coils provide one spark per cycle, which is efficient for standard combustion engines. In contrast, multi-spark ignition coils deliver multiple sparks within a single cycle, enhancing fuel atomization and improving combustion efficiency. This can lead to better engine performance, reduced emissions, and improved fuel economy. As automotive technology advances, both types cater to specific performance needs and engine designs.</p></p>
