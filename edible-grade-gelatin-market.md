<p><strong>The Edible Grade Gelatin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acid Hydrolysis</li><li>Alkaline Hydrolysis</li><li>Enzyme Preparation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The edible grade gelatin market is segmented based on the method of preparation: acid hydrolysis, alkaline hydrolysis, and enzyme preparation. Acid hydrolysis involves treating collagen-rich substrates with acids, resulting in a quick breakdown of gel strength and lower viscosity. Alkaline hydrolysis uses bases for a gradual breakdown, yielding higher gel strength and a more robust texture. Enzyme preparation utilizes specific enzymes to selectively hydrolyze collagen, offering a milder approach that preserves flavor and nutritional quality, catering to diverse applications in food and beverages.</p></p>
