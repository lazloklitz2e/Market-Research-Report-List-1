<p><ul><li>Explosion-Proof Centrifugal Fan</li><li>Explosion-Proof Axial Flow Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The explosion-proof fan market primarily consists of two types: explosion-proof centrifugal fans and explosion-proof axial flow fans. </p><p>Explosion-proof centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to create high-pressure airflow, making them ideal for ventilating hazardous areas with explosive gases or dust. In contrast, explosion-proof axial flow fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, providing high-volume airflow for larger spaces. Both designs are engineered to prevent ignition sources and ensure safe operation in volatile environments, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
