<p><p>Biometric Point-of-Sale (POS) terminals enhance transaction security by using unique biological traits for identification. Fingerprint identification captures and matches fingerprints, while palm-vein identification scans the unique vein patterns in a palm. Facial recognition analyzes facial features, and iris recognition focuses on the unique patterns in the eye's iris. Retina recognition scans the blood vessel patterns in the retina, offering high accuracy. Voice recognition uses vocal characteristics for authentication, making these biometric methods robust against fraud and enhancing customer trust in transactions.</p></p>
