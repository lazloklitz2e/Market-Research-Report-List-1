<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Temperature Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Non-distributed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic temperature monitor market comprises two primary types: distributed and non-distributed monitoring. Distributed temperature sensing (DTS) utilizes fiber optic cables to measure temperature changes along the entire length of the cable, allowing for continuous monitoring over large areas. In contrast, non-distributed monitoring uses discrete fiber optic sensors at specific locations to monitor temperature, making it suitable for targeted applications. Both types offer unique advantages in industries such as oil and gas, power generation, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
